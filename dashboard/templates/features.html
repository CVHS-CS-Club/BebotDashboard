Features

Bebot has many different commands for you to use.

Moderation Commands

  • b.clear for clearing messages
  • b.nuke for getting rid of up to 1000 messages
  • b.kick for kicking users
  • b.ban for banning users
  • b.unban for unbanning users
  • b.mute for muting users
  • b.unmute for unmuting users
  • b.welcome for setting up a welcome message
  • b.cursemod for setting a profanity filter
Learn more »

XP Commands

  • b.level to get your level
Learn more »

Anime Commands

  • b.anisearch to get details on an anime
  • b.anistream to get an anime stream
  • b.watchbebop to get an episode of cowboy bebop
Learn more »

Economy Commands

  • b.daily to get your daily 5000 woolongs
  • b.tohand to send woolongs from the bank to the hand
  • b.tobank to send woolongs from the hand to the bank
  • b.bj to play BlackJack
  • b.gamblenums to play the number game
Learn more »

Gaming Commands

  • b.mw to get your COD MW stats
  • b.fortnite to get your fortnite stats
Learn more »

Music Commands

  • b.join to join your VC
  • b.leave to leave its VC
  • b.play to play a song or playlist from a search or url
  • b.loop to loop the queue
  • b.queue to get the current queue
  • b.pause to pause the playing song
  • b.unpause to unpause the playing song
  • b.volume to set bebots volume
